Feature 1

Diet stretching

Bring your palms together in front of your chest and then extend your arms above your head. Interlace your fingers, extend your index fingers and reach up with your arms and entire upper body. Keep your gaze steady on the flame. Breathe deeply and calmly. At least four breaths.

Feature 2

Shoulder & chest stretch

Release your hands and bring your arms back, down&t. Place your palms about half a meter behind your body. Push your chest forward in your hips, bring your shoulders together and point your chin up. Close your eyes and take four deep, calm breaths..

Feature 3

Back health & balance

After the back comes the curve, for the good of the back! Bring bent legs up towards the body, arch the back and grip the back of the thighs. Find the balance. Chin slightly downwards, but preferably with eyes towards the fire. Four deep, calm breaths..
